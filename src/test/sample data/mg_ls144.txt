Alabama Auctions Daily Summary
Alabama livestock auctions that were held Tuesday May 03, 2016.

Cattle Receipts: 2,252   Last Week: 2,587   Last Year: 2,657 

  Compared to one week ago: Slaughter cows and bulls sold 
steady to 3.00 higher. All feeder classes sold steady to 4.00 
lower. Trade moderate with moderate demand on feeders. 
Replacement cows and pairs sold mostly steady. Slaughter cows 
made up 10 percent of the offerings, bulls 2 percent, 
replacement cows and pairs 13 percent, and feeders 75 percent. 
The feeder supply included 19 percent steers, 40 percent 
heifers, and 41 percent bulls. Near 18 percent of the run 
weighed over 600 lbs.
